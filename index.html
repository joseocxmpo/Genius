Something to give eachother - Troye Sivan

Something To Give Each Other is the highly anticipated third studio album by Australian singer-songwriter, Troye Sivan, released on October 13, 2023. This album serves as Sivan’s musical comeback since his acclaimed 2018 album Bloom and the EP In A Dream (2020).

Melodrama - Lorde

Melodrama is Lorde’s sophomore album, released on June 16, 2017.
Inspired in part by the “emotions and insights” gained after her breakup with longtime boyfriend, Lorde calls Melodrama “a record about being alone… The good parts and the bad parts”.

MOTOMAMI+ - Rosalía

MOTOMAMI is the name of the third studio album by ROSALÍA, released on March 18, 2022.
